Pattern Making Workflow

Design Analysis

Study the design sketch, analyze construction details, and determine the most efficient pattern making approach for the garment.

1

Measurements & Calculations

Take accurate body measurements, calculate ease allowances, and determine the base size for pattern development.

2

Basic Block Creation

Draft the basic sloper or block pattern that serves as the foundation for the specific design modifications.

3

Style Line Development

Add design details, style lines, darts, and shape modifications to transform the basic block into the desired design.

4

Pattern Testing & Fitting

Create a test garment (muslin or toile) to evaluate fit, make necessary adjustments, and refine the pattern.

5

Final Pattern Preparation

Add seam allowances, notches, grainlines, and all necessary markings for production-ready patterns.

6

Grading & Production

Grade the pattern across size ranges and prepare technical packages for manufacturing or further development.

7
