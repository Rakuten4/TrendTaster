Digital Design Workflow

1

Concept Development

Initial sketching and mood board creation using digital illustration tools to establish design direction and aesthetic vision.

2

Pattern Creation

Digital pattern making with precise measurements, grading across sizes, and technical flat drawings for production reference.

3

3D Visualization

Creating virtual 3D garments with realistic fabric simulation to visualize fit, drape, and overall design before physical production.

4

Virtual Fitting

Testing garments on various avatar body types to optimize fit, identify issues, and refine designs for different demographics.

5

Marketing Renders

Producing high-quality photorealistic renders for catalogs, e-commerce, and marketing materials without physical photoshoots.

6

Production Files

Generating technical packages, size specifications, and production-ready files for efficient manufacturing processes.
